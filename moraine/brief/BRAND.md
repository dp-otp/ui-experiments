# MORAINE — Brand Bible

## Name

**MORAINE** — *n.* the rocky sediment left behind by a glacier; the residue of slow, monumental force.

7 letters. Two syllables. One word. Geological term, rare in fashion. Ownable as `.com` neighborhood and across socials.

## Tagline

> Garments deposited, not designed.

## Positioning (one sentence)

Moraine releases numbered "deposits" of six garments, catalogued like museum specimens, made to outlast the buyer and be inherited.

## Archetype

Sediment. Inheritance. Monumental. The brand world is quarries, glaciers, ironstone, salt flats, lichen, slow time. Clothing is treated as deposit, not product — an accumulation that survives generational handover, not a seasonal release.

## Differentiators

1. **No seasons.** Numbered deposits replace SS/AW. Each deposit is six garments, limited, never restocked.
2. **Specimen-card cataloguing.** Every piece carries a code (DM-04-01), a stratum reference, a fabric weight, and an edition number. Marketing copy reads like a museum specimen card, not a fashion ad.
3. **Inheritance program.** Lifetime free repair, in perpetuity, for any owner — including those who inherited a Moraine piece from someone else. Each garment carries a stitched-in logbook for repair entries and life events.
4. **Mineral-derived palette.** No Pantone references. Every color is named after a specific specimen (Talus Grey, Hematite Bloom, Iceland Spar, Drift Salt). Each season's palette is drawn from a real mineral specimen, photographed and named on the tag.

## Voice

Cataloguing, archival, weighty, restrained. Reads like a geological dossier or museum label. Cold, observational, declarative.

**Banned words** (zero tolerance): luxury, timeless, elevated, curated, iconic, essential, modern, minimalist, sleek, refined, introducing, in a world where, we believe, you, your.

**Permitted register**: physical (weight, drape, hand, gauge), provenance (mill, finish, era, stratum), process (mordanted, salt-bleached, hand-loomed), permanence (outlast, inherit, repair, deposit).

## Typography (locked)

- **Display / wordmark**: Fraunces — Black weight, Display optical size (144), high-contrast. Used at large scale for headlines and the wordmark itself.
- **Technical labels / specimen data**: IBM Plex Mono — 400/500. All-caps for specimen codes, sentence-case for tabular data.
- **Body copy**: Bricolage Grotesque — variable, 400–600. Used for editorial captions, philosophy paragraphs, footer.

Zero Inter. Zero Tailwind grey. Zero gradient hero. Zero stock illustration.

## Cataloguing system

```
DM-NN-XX
│  │  └─ piece number within deposit (01–06)
│  └─── deposit number (ascending, never resets)
└────── deposit prefix
```

A garment ID also carries a stratum line on the tag — a one-line geological annotation pointing to the inspiration source, written like a specimen plate caption.

Example: `DM-04-01 · Quaternary scree, Hebridean coast, A.D. 2024 · 28oz Italian melton wool · of 84`

## Inheritance program (the brand promise)

Every Moraine garment carries a stitched-in logbook — a small notebook in a hidden pocket where the owner records repairs, hand-overs, and the life the cloth has touched. Moraine repairs every piece without charge, in perpetuity, for any owner. Pieces inherited from another wearer are entered into the archive on receipt; the archive entry transfers, the warranty does not lapse.

## Distribution

Direct-to-consumer first. No wholesale until Deposit 06+. No discounting, ever. No paid social before Deposit 03+. Primary channel: direct waitlist + considered editorial press.

## What Moraine is NOT

- Not minimalist. Maximalist of weight, density, provenance.
- Not unisex by accident. Each piece is cut deliberately — some for one form, some for either.
- Not heritage-cosplay. Doesn't pretend to be 1920s workwear or Edwardian tailoring.
- Not "quiet luxury". The brand has a loud philosophy; the clothes are quiet.
- Not seasonal. Drops are unpredictable in date but predictable in form.
